Grid Intro

  • What is Grid.
  • More powerful than flexbox.
  • Used for more complex layouts.
  • Uses rows and columns.
  • Items inside are told where they are positioned in each row and column.
  • Items can span multiple columns or rows.
  • Works well with flexbox and media queries.
