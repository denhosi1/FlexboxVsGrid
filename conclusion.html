Conclusion

  • No longer need to use things such as display table, floats etc to force layouts.
  • Flexbox is good for simple designs and single axis layouts.
  • Use grid for more complex layouts.
  • Grid mixed with flexbox can add even more flexibility to your layouts.
  • Blended with media queries gives you even more power.
  • Unfortunately grid is not fully supported yet, but it is getting very close.
