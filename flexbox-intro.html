Flexbox Intro

  • What is Flexbox.
  • No more needing floats for layouts.
  • Two types of elements - Container and Items.
  • Set container to Display: flex to begin.
  • Add wrapping, justify content etc.
  • Order, Flex-Grow and Alignment properties on each item.
  • Blend with media queries to gain further control.
